Extra Curricular

Newburgh Armory Unity Center

I am a facilitator at the Computer Science Program hosted at Newburgh Armory Unity Center, a recreational center where people of all ages, races and religions are welcome to participate in civic unity. I teach structural programing to the young, underserved students of Newburgh. There are a selected number of kids who are accepted into the program, typically second to eighth graders. This experience has allowed not only the youth of Newburgh to improve their critical thinking and problem solving skills, but also me. This program has opened up my eyes to recognize the positive impact that I can create in my community.

Community Service

I am also dedicated to my community. Aside from teaching structural programming and coding to underserved youth at our local Newburgh Armory Unity Center, I am also a founding member of Global to Local and serve on the Excelsior Academy Awareness Committee. Global to Local is a student-led global service learning program in which students fundraise to volunteer overseas and then return to our city to act as Project Managers for local, community-based impact projects. All of these endeavors show my commitment to both social equity and volunteerism.
